Do you or someone you know have Charcot-Marie-Tooth Disease? Or maybe you are looking to learn more about different diseases. Whether you or someone you know has C.M.T. or you are looking to learn something new, this page will help you to understand what Charcot Marie-Tooth Disease is and how it affects those who have it.

I was diagnosed with Charcot Marie-Tooth Disease and I am writing this page to help people become more aware of C.M.T. also known as Charcot-Marie-Tooth Disease. C.M.T. affects millions of people worldwide, however, not many people know about C.M.T. or what it is, making it a rare disease.
